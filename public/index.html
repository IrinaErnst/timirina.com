Tim was born in Arizona, raised in Fullerton, California and attended the University of North Carolina at Charlotte where he studied economics. His first job out of school landed him in New York and he's been living in New York for the past five years.

Irina- Kalashnikova -

Irina was born and raised in Izhevsk, Russia. She studied Economics at Izhevsk State University before moving to Moscow. She took a leap of faith when she moved to New York to follow her dreams. This past January she received her second University degree, in computer science, from CUNY Hunter College in Manhattan.

OUR STORY

Tim and Irina both moved to New York City in the Fall of 2010. Separately. Irina moved to the United States from Izhevsk, Russia. She had been visiting her sister Natasha in San Franscisco and decided to stay a few weeks longer to visit New York City and a good friend from her home town. It was New York that she first fell in love with. As soon as she could she started school at 317 Madison Ave next to Grand Central Terminal.

Meanwhile, Tim started a new job in finance and began his career as well. It so happens he also worked at 317 Madison Ave. For two years the two of them were walking a similar route, riding in the same elevators, but never once did they meet. After two years of missed opportunities Irina was accepted into a degree program studying computer science at Hunter College. She soon attended her final class at 317 Madison. It seemed that perhaps they would never meet.

Finally, it was time for fate to intervene. Unbeknownst to them both, their mutual friends Vandi & Greg were conspiring. They thought these two eligible singles should meet and that they were meant to be together. They seemed to know what a great couple they would be. So, after years of crossed paths, Tim & Irina finally did discover each other.

You know what? Vandi and Greg were right.

THE EVENTS

We are really excited to share with you the locations we have chosen for the events in our wedding. We'll start with an age-old, and fun, Russian tradition, follow it with the most beautiful of mountain-top weddings, and end the day with a celebration with the best people in the world!

THE BRIDE'S RANSOM

The Bride's Ransom, known in Russian as Выкуп невесты (Vykup nevesty), is perhaps one of the oldest Russian wedding traditions. Its main purpose is to have a good bit of fun, mostly at the Groom's expense. The Bridesmaids are in charge of lightly tormenting Tim until he gives in and pays his 'ransom'. Its an event you won't want to miss if you'd like to see Tim squirm a little.

WEDDING CEREMONY @ THE RIDGE

The Ridge provides guests with breathtaking 360-degree views of North Carolina's gorgeous mountain ranges. Located in Marshall, NC, twenty-five miles north of Asheville, The Ridge offers a sense of rustic elegance while providing the perfect backdrop to our special day. After the ceremony, enjoy cocktails and appetizers on the residence’s wraparound porch or take a walk around the lush property complete with rolling hills and friendly horses.

RECEPTION @ PACK'S TAVERN, THE CENTURY ROOM

The Century Room offers a beautiful turn of the century ballroom overlooking Pack Square Park and the Park’s amphitheatre. High ceilings, exposed wood trusses and brick walls will create a wonderful atmosphere for our celebration. With a backdrop of downtown Asheville, we'll enjoy wonderful food, impeccable service, and plenty of room for dancing! You are prepared for dancing right?
